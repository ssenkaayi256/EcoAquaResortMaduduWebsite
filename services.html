Our Services

ECOAQUA RESORT MADUDU offers a range of professional services designed to meet the needs of our clients while promoting sustainable practices. Our core services are detailed below.

Consultancy

We provide expert consultancy in various fields, including baseline surveys, research, business plan development, and comprehensive project planning and management. Our goal is to help businesses and organizations establish solid foundations for their projects and achieve their strategic objectives.

Project Evaluations

Our team conducts thorough mid-term and final project evaluations to assess performance, identify areas for improvement, and ensure project goals are met efficiently. We provide detailed reports and actionable insights to guide future decisions.

Aquaculture Farming & Ecotourism

We specialize in both aquaculture farming and ecotourism. Our work in aquaculture focuses on sustainable fish farming practices. In ecotourism, we develop and manage eco-friendly resorts that offer unique experiences while preserving the natural environment.
